# IELTS Mock Test - Media Files Structure

Bu papkada IELTS mock test uchun barcha media fayllar saqlanadi.

## Struktura

### Listening
- **listening/audio/** - Listening audio fayllari
  - section1/ - Section 1 audio fayllari
  - section2/ - Section 2 audio fayllari
  - section3/ - Section 3 audio fayllari
  - section4/ - Section 4 audio fayllari

- **listening/images/** - Listening savollardagi rasmlar (agar kerak bo'lsa)
  - section1/ - Section 1 rasmlari
  - section2/ - Section 2 rasmlari
  - section3/ - Section 3 rasmlari
  - section4/ - Section 4 rasmlari

### Reading
- **reading/images/** - Reading passagelardagi rasmlar, diagrammalar
  - passage1/ - Passage 1 rasmlari
  - passage2/ - Passage 2 rasmlari
  - passage3/ - Passage 3 rasmlari

### Writing
- **writing/images/** - Writing task rasmlari (grafiklar, jadvallar, diagrammalar)
  - task1/ - Task 1 rasmlari (grafik, jadval, diagram)
  - task2/ - Task 2 rasmlari (agar kerak bo'lsa)

### Speaking
- **speaking/audio/** - Speaking test audio fayllari (faqat AI Speaking uchun)
  - part1/ - Part 1 audio
  - part2/ - Part 2 audio
  - part3/ - Part 3 audio

## Fayl Nomlash

Audio fayllar:
- `section1_001.mp3`, `section2_001.mp3`, ...

Rasm fayllar:
- `section1_001_q1.jpg`, `passage1_001_diagram.png`, ...
- `task1_001_chart.jpg`, `task1_002_table.png`, ...

## Format Talablari

- **Audio**: MP3 format, 128-192 kbps
- **Images**: JPG, PNG format, optimal sifat va hajm
